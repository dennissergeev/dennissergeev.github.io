Together with Tiago Silva and [Maria Zamyatina](https://mariazamyatina.wordpress.com/about/), we improved the [last year's](teaching/2016-11-17-enveast-python-course) Python Course and ran it with support from [EnvEast DTP](http://www.enveast.ac.uk/) and [Cefas](http://www.cefas.co.uk/).
The course started with introduction to [Python](http://www.python.org/) programming language, proceeding to basics of the analysis and visualisation of geosciences data.
Overall, the course was a great success, as the total number of attendees reached 40.

The course content is freely available as
* static notebooks on [nbviewer](http://nbviewer.jupyter.org/github/ueapy/pythoncourse2018-materials/blob/master/index.ipynb)
* or interactive notebooks in the cloud [![Binder](http://mybinder.org/badge.svg)](http://mybinder.org:/repo/ueapy/pythoncourse2018-materials)
